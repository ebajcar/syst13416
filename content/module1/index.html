In this module...
  • Understand and describe computing environments and their key components. Overview basics of computer hardware and software. Discuss background and philosophy of computers.
  • Acknowledge responsibilities and computer use concerns.
  • Explain what is an operating system and what are the common ones used today.
  • Contrast open source and proprietarycode.
  • Explain the role of international standards in computing.
  • Contrast the use of GUI and CLI. Distinguish between client and server environment.
  • Discuss Unix/Linux operating system, its role and components. Summarize the history of the UNIX Operating System. Compare characteristics of UNIX with other OS. Illustrate features and advantages of using the UNIX OS.
  • Describe Linux Account and what it provides.
  • Use remote login to log on and off the computer network.
  • Understand and use the following commands: ssh login bash sh csh tcsh ksh logoutexitsusudopasswdman
